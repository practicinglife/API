ConnectWise PSA Term Definition Synonyms Examples
General
ConnectWise PSA Central ConnectWise PSA Central provides you with links to useful resources. Links to various educational and support-oriented resources are listed in one convenient location. Clicking a link opens the page in your default internet browser.    
ConnectWise PSA Today This screen populates each time you log in to ConnectWise PSA . It contains usable info such as quick links, KPIs, schedule detail, approvals, and much more.  Home Page  
Contact These are the clients, prospects, vendors and even internal staff that you enter data for. This includes service tickets, activities, contact info, etc.  Client, Customer, End User, Prospect  
Member These are the users of your ConnectWise PSA instance. They have been added to the Member Setup under the System module, and they have access to the data stored in your system. User, Employee  
Module An area of ConnectWise PSA . The system is organized based on the functions performed within each module. Left hand navigation lists out the different ConnectWise PSA modules. Navigation section Favorites, Companies, Sales, Marketing, Procurement, Project, Service Desk, Time and Expenses, Finance, and System 
My Favorites The top module in the ConnectWise PSA navigation pane. This module houses the ConnectWise PSA Today  and the ConnectWise PSA Central Screens by default. However, it can be customized to contain any screens you wish to have quick and easy access to. Bookmarks My Calendar, Service Board, Dispatch Portal
Picker This icon allows you to perform searches for items that do not populate in the dropdown menu or do not have a dropdown option.  Magnifying glass  
Service
Closed Loop Closed Loop is a way to continuously update and provide feedback to both the customer and the internal staff while documenting it all within the Detail Description field of the service ticket. All communication uses the Email Connector set up for your system. Communication loop, Email thread  
Detailed Description The Detailed Description should contain all notes and work being completed on a service ticket. The details entered here are customer facing, and closed loop communication will also populate here. Notes  
Internal Analysis The Internal Analysis should be used for internal notes only. Time entry notes should not be entered here as these notes are not visible to the customer via the customer portal or the invoice.    
Item Service Ticket Item is the most granular level of categorization for tickets within your system. It controls what settings are applied to a ticket via an Auto Template. Subcategory Add, Edit, Remove, Install, 
Knowledge Base The Knowledge Base allows you to search your database for information that you have entered previously. You may search for key words in service tickets, project tickets, project issues, Knowledge Base Articles, time entries and activities.  Self Service Portal Resolutions to frequent issues, process documents, 
Resolution The Resolution is similar to the Detailed Description, but can be used to enter the final time entry that includes the resolution to the service ticket. This can then be used for review in the future.    
Scheduled Resource Members assigned to work a ticket. The member/s will be included in the closed loop communication with the contact, and workflow and notifications can be implemented using the resources.  Assigned members, Resources, Employees Assigned Technician  
Service Board The Service Board is where all tickets are located within your system. All service boards are attached to a location/group combination, and security can be built around these settings. Automation can also be tied to a service board, and service board settings can be configured for the tickets on each board.  Ticket queue, Work Group Help Desk, Admin, RMM, Sales, Billing/Accounting
Service Template Service Templates allow you to format details on a ticket template, and they can be used to modify or append information on a Service Ticket. They can also be used to create Recurring Service Tickets for a specific Company or Agreement.   Onsite maintenance, Recurring business reviews, Workstation Installs
Service Ticket Service tickets are records of a service requests. Tickets are attached to companies and contacts including internal items. Service tickets allow you to track work, schedule resources and document resolutions. Automation and proactive monitoring can also be set up. Service Request, Work Order Error messages, Alerts, Installs, Meetings, 
Status Service Ticket Statuses are used to determine the stage of a ticket as it flows through its lifecycle. Status also control SLAs, email communication to customers, workflow rules, and whether a ticket is open or closed.    New, Closed, In Progress, Waiting
Subtype Service Ticket Subtype is the second level of categorization for tickets within your system. It controls what items can be selected, and reports can be used to determine the amount of ticket submitted and hours worked against a certain Type and Subtype of ticket. A type, subtype, and item combination can also automate the ticket triaging process using Auto Templates. Category Window, Exchange, SQL, Firewall, Security
Summary Summary of the issue. It is a ConnectWise PSA best practice to be as explicit as possible in this summary because it acts as a point of reference in many different places throughout the application. Subject "My computer won't turn on", "I have a new employee", "Onsite Maintenance Visit"
Tasks Tasks are steps or processes resources should complete as they are working a ticket. Tasks ensure all required processes are completed as well as consistency. To Do Items "Install Operating System as directed by quote", "Add workstation to the domain and configure the profile", "Install RMM Agent (which will push A/V and Backup solution)", 
Type Service Ticket Type is the highest level of categorization for tickets within your system. It controls what subtypes can be selected, and reports can be used to determine the amount of ticket submitted and hours worked against a certain type of ticket. A type, subtype, and item combination can also automate the ticket triaging process using Auto Templates.   Workstation, Server, Phone, Printer/Copier, Application
Project
Closed Loop Closed Loop is a way to continuously update and provide feedback to both the customer and the internal staff while documenting it all within the Detail Description field of the service ticket. All communication uses the Email Connector set up for your system.    
Detailed Description The Detailed Description should contain all notes and work being completed on a project ticket. The details entered here are customer facing, and closed loop communication will also populate here. Notes  
Internal Analysis The Internal Analysis should be used for internal notes only. Time entry notes should not be entered here as these notes are not visible to the customer via the customer portal or the invoice. Internal notes  
Issue Ticket Issue Tickets are created to help track those items that may have been created as an afterthought or that have any outstanding questions. Project Tickets can be converted to Issue Tickets and vice versa.  Out of scope ticket Dropped hardware and need to order new, Changes in demands from customer
Phase Phases help organize and bill projects. Sub phases and project tickets can be created under phases in the hierarchy of the workplan, and billing options can be customized for each phase of a project.    Project Management, Installation and Configuration, Admin Orientation, Out of Scope
Project Board Project Boards provide summary views of projects by location and group. Security can be implemented on service board, and automation can be customized on a per board basis.    Onboarding, Development, Internal
Project Status Project Status is used to distinguish the stage of the lifecycle the project is in. This can be used for proactive monitoring with workflow rules, and should not be confused with Project Ticket Status. Project phase New, In Progress, Closed, On Hold 
Project Template Project Templates are workplans that can be applied to existing projects. You can map out the phases, sub phases, and tickets to reflect your normal workflow, and then reference the template on projects.  Project outline Server migrations, client onboarding, employee onboarding
Project Ticket Project Tickets are created, assigned, and worked to complete project pieces. They are organized by phases, and they can be used to schedule resources and establish due dates. Project tasks  
Project Type Project Type is used to categorize the work being done on a project. The type also allows you to split revenue based. Examples include design or installation.  Project Category Install, Migration, Website
Resolution The Resolution is similar to the Detailed Description, but can be used to enter the final time entry that includes the resolution to the project ticket. This can then be used for review in the future.    
Scheduled Resource Members assigned to work a ticket. The member/s will be included in the closed loop communication with the contact, and workflow and notifications can be implemented using the resources.  Assigned members, responsible members  
Summary Summary of the work being completed. It is a ConnectWise PSA best practice to be as explicit as possible in this summary because it acts as a point of reference in many different places throughout the application. Subject line  
Tasks Tasks are steps or processes resources should complete as they are working a ticket. Tasks ensure all required processes are completed as well as consistency. To Do Items  
Work Plan The Project Work Plan tab displays the organization of the Project. Projects are divided into phases, sub-phases and tickets. Project plan, project outline  
Finance
Accounting Interface The Accounting Interface screen is used to configure the information that will carry over to your accounting package.    QuickBooks, Great Plains
Agreement Agreements are used to automate your billing process. Often recurring, they allow you to track your customer hours and bill accordingly for possible overages. Contracts Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Block time
Agreement Invoicing The Agreement Invoice section of the invoicing screen is where all recurring billable items will populate. Details from the agreements will populate here based on the recurring cycle setup.     
Batch Number A Batch Number is the unique identification number assigned to a set of items you are exporting to your accounting package. The batch number can be used to review items sent over in that export as well as reverse a set of exported items.    "01012015_1" = First batch transferred on 1/1/2015)
Billing Status The Billing Status determines the phase in the invoice lifecycle an invoice is in. The status also controls which statuses are considered closed and can be batched over to the accounting package. Invoice Status New, Review Needed, Closed (Email), Closed (Mail)
Credit Memo A Credit Memo is a special invoice that enables you to issue a credit to your customer. Credit memos can also be applied to invoices within your system to offset the receivable amount. Credit Crediting for services/labor, Crediting for products
Direct Transfer Direct Transfer is the integration setup with your accounting package. It allows information to be directly exported into the accounting system you are using(Some Limitations).    
Downpayment Invoice A Downpayment is used to collect a prepaid portion of a job upfront in order to improve cash flow. Deposit, Upfront invoice Requiring % of total up front
Email Template Email Templates are sent to your customers when distributing invoices via email. They can be customized to include your company information as well as the clients details. The invoice is then attached as a PDF to the email template sent.  Invoice Email Layout, HTML Template  
Invoice Invoices are sent to customers for services rendered. These can be for Service Tickets, Projects, Agreements, Sales Orders, or miscellaneous items. Billing statement, bill, statement of charges  
Invoice Batch Emailing The Invoice Batch Emailing screen is used to mass email invoices to your customers. The email template received by your customers can be customized before being sent as well.    
Invoice Template Invoice Templates are used to customize the format of your invoices. This includes the labels, captions, and also what does and doesn't show on the invoice sent to the customer. Invoice Layout, Invoice format Default, Project, Block Agreement, Altered Margins
Invoicing Wizard The Invoice Wizard is where all non-recurring items go when they are in a ready to bill status.   Fixed Fee service tickets, Project Billing (fixed fee or actual rates), time, expense and products
Miscellaneous Invoice Miscellaneous invoices are used for billing miscellaneous totals. For example, product sales that are not applied to a Sales Order, Service Ticket, or Project. One-off invoice Storefront purchases, Quick invoices for abnormal items
My Invoices The My Invoices Screen works with security roles and routing rules to allow you to give your employees access to only the invoices that you want them to see.    
Sales
Close Date Enter an estimated close date to map your sales quota. End Date  
Estimate Estimate line items allow you to enter quotable items that do not exist in your ConnectWise PSA product database. These are free form text fields that will not convert to sales orders, service tickets, or projects.    One-off quoted items,
Forecast The Forecast contains items you wish to potentially sell to your customer. This information can be printed and used as a quote. Quote  
Opportunity Opportunities drive the sales recommendations through the system. You are able to manage, quote, automate, and convert the sales leads that your company controls. Quote New Customers, Upgrading Existing Customers
Product Products pull from the existing list in your database, and these items will transfer from the opportunity to a sales order, service ticket, or project once the opportunity is marked as "won". Item Hardware, Software, Labor, Agreement Items
Sales Activity Sales Activities are tasks that can be assigned to resources, scheduled, and have time and expenses entered against it.  To Do's Email Customer, Call Contact, Lunch with Customer
Sales Rep The Sales Rep is the primary owner of the opportunity. This label can be updated on the My Company Owner Tab.    
Sales Stage Sales Stage determines what phase of the sales process the opportunity is in. The sales stage can also update the probability of winning the opportunity.   Lead, Quoted, Committed, Won, Qualified
Secondary Sales Rep The Secondary Sales Rep is the second owner of the opportunity. This label can be updated on the My Company Owner Tab.    
Status Opportunity Status denotes the lifecycle of the opportunity.    New, On Hold, Quote Sent, Quote Approved, Closed
Track Tracks can be added to companies, contacts, and opportunities. They automate process based on a timeline you set up based on the date you attach the track.    New Sales Lead, Collections, Onboarding
Companies
Account This field provides a cross-reference to match the company to its counterpart in your accounting package.  It must match the customer name or ID in your accounting package.    
Company and Contact Import A lead import allows you to mass import companies and contacts. The import process also allows the option to automate the creation of sales activities, the addition of a group, and also attach a track.  Lead Import Initial import of companies/contacts, lead lists, calling lists, Tradeshow attendee list
Company ID Enter an abbreviated name for the Company that will be displayed on screens with mouse over functionality. The ID is to serve as a unique identifier in the case where you have more than one company with the same name. Company Code  
Configuration Configurations are form templates that allow you to customize the data you wish to store about a specific item. They can also be automatically created through the API for devices you may manage. Device, Asset Workstations, Services, Printers, Copiers, Budgets, Software, Hardware
Site Sites are used for customers that have more than one location. You can enter details specific to the location and assign contacts that belong to that particular site. Office, Customer location Main office, Branch office, Franchise locations
Team The team is a role based setup that designates the responsible individuals both internal and onsite. Both members and contacts can be associated to a team role.    Account Manager, Account Tech, Key Contact, Emergency Contact
Vendor Xref This field is only required for the Purchasing and Inventory add-on modules.  The vendor name or id from the accounting package must be entered here for item receipts to post properly.    
Scheduling
Dispatch Portal The Dispatch Portal is a comprehensive view of all scheduled service tickets, project tickets, activities, and scheduled appointments. The view can be customized to see member details, service tickets, and project tickets. Items can then be dragged and dropped to assign.  Team Calendar, Team Schedule Drag and Drop unassigned tickets
My Calendar The My Calendar screen is the calendar view of service tickets, project tickets and activities that are assigned to a specific date and time, or only a date.  My Schedule, My Calendar  
Scheduled Resource Members assigned to work a ticket. The member/s will be included in the closed loop communication with the contact, and workflow and notifications can be implemented using the resources.  Assigned members, responsible members  
Marketing
Groups Groups allow the ability to segment the contacts in my system. These segments can then be used for mass emails, activity creation, and the attachment of tracks.  Target Markets Referrals, Holiday Card, Newsletter, Leads
Historical Activity Historical Activities are closed activities usually denoting that an action was taken through the marketing manager.  Historical Record Emails Sent, Tasks Attached to Companies
Marketing Campaign Marketing Campaign enables your marketing function to manage and evaluate the effectiveness of your company's marketing efforts. ROI, emails opened, activities created, and many other metrics can also be tracked using marketing campaigns. Marketing efforts Lunch and Learns, Website, Lead Lists, Referrals, Conventions
Marketing Manager The Marketing Manager enables the ability to send an email, activity creation, Word mail merge, and assignment of tracks to each of the contacts selected.  Mail merge Marketing Email Blasts, Mass Outage Emails, Mas activity creation,
Status The Campaign Status denotes what stage in the lifecycle a marketing campaign is in.    Open, Closed, On Hold, Cancelled
Subtype Marketing Subtype allows you to classify what type of campaign that you are setting up. This information can then be used for reporting and determining which marketing endeavors are the most lucrative.  Marketing Subcategory Referral, Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4, Lunch and Learn
Type Marketing Type allows you to classify what type of campaign that you are setting up. This information can then be used for reporting and determining which marketing endeavors are the most lucrative.  Marketing Category Seminar, Paid Search, Website, Email
Procurement
Adjustment Type The type of inventory adjustment.    Used in house, Stolen, Initial Count
Pricing Schedule A list of pricing rules for items that are sold. There may be different pricing schedules for a particular customer or group of customers.    Non-profit pricing, discounts for bulk purchasing (5% discount for each 100qty clients purchase)
Purchase Order A purchase order is a document issued by a buyer to a seller. It is a list of products and/or services that details agreed upon prices that the seller will deliver to the buyer. A PO is seen as a contract between the buyer and seller. PO, Order Number  
Purchase Qty The Purchase Qty (Quantity) displays the current demand for the product.  The partner is able to override the purchase quantity if more or less of a product is needed.    When researching pricing, the purchasing agent discovers that the full quantity of product is not available at Vendor A.  The purchasing agent can update the purchase quantity and create a purchase order for the product that is available from Vendor A.  The remainder can then be purchased from an alternate vendor.
Purchasing Agent The person responsible for the purchase of goods and supplies for an organization. The purchasing agent collects all the orders and checks pricing and availability for the items that are on order. They also may be responsible to identify price breaks and rebates that are available to the company.    
Purchasing Approvals The process by which items are approved for purchasing. The reason a partner may use the approval process is to help out with cash flow. They may use a Just in time method of purchasing and may want to wait until the last moment to order an item.    
Receiving Checking the purchase order to verify that all items have been received from the vendor. This helps to ensure that all items are on hand when it is time to deliver to the customer.    
RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) An RMA is a transaction between the recipient of the product and the seller or manufacturer of that product. The manufacturer or seller issues an RMA number to help them identify the returned item. This RMA number is used to track the transaction. It is an intention to replace or repair the item.   Malfunctioning warranted products
Sales Order An order received by a business from a customer. Sales orders are internal documents. They may be created from the quote that was sent to the customer or from the customers purchase order. A sales order is used to track the customers order from inception to invoicing. SO,   
Serialized Inventory These are products that are kept on hand that are kept track of by serial numbers. When using serialized inventory, it is important to track which serial number went to which customer.    
Shipping This is when the Inventory item is delivered to the customer.     
Special Order Special Overrides the demand calculation allowing the full complement of products for a customer to be purchased regardless of the current Stock on Hand count.    
Vendor Purchase Order This is the purchase order that the partner will create from the ConnectWise PSA system that lists the items that they intend to buy from the vendor they are placing an order with. This PO lists the costs for the items they are buying to sell to their customer.    
Warehouse A location where products are stored.   Locations, Internal Offices, Room
Warehouse Bin A sub-location in the warehouse.    Shelf, Box, Vehicle Trunk
Time and Expense
Billable Time accrued is billable to the customer. It will transfer to the invoicing wizard unless covered/overridden in some method.   Remote work, Onsite work, After Hours work
Charge Code Charge Codes are used for internal types of time tracking. Reporting can be completed to determine the amount of internal time entered per member.   Admin, Lunch, Vacation, Holiday, Training
Classification The Classification of expenses control the type of reimbursement.   Reimbursable, Non-Reimbursable, Personal
Closed Loop Closed Loop Time Entry is a method of updating the customer, notes, status, time, and expenses of a ticket from a single popup window. Communication loop, Email thread  
Do Not Bill Time accrued is NOT billable to the customer and no invoice is generated.    
Expense Report Expense Reports display all expense entries that are made for a specific member for a selected date range. Expense Sheet Monthly, Weekly,
Expense Type Expense Types categorize the expenses being entered within your system.    Mileage, Meals, Transportation, Hotel/Motel
No Charge Time accrued is NOT billable to the customer, but a zero dollar invoice is generated. No Charge is often used when you want to show the customer work being performed, but no charge is applied.   Admin time, sales time
Non-Reimbursable Items that are marked non-reimbursable will show in the Company Paid Expenses section on the member's expense report. They can be billable to the customer, but will not be included in the reimbursement amount.    Expenses paid by company credit card.
Personal Items that are marked as personal will be listed as negative amounts on the expense report.   Personal expenses charged against company credit card
Reimbursable Items that are marked as reimbursable will display in the Employee Paid Expenses section on the member's expense report. They are also included in the reimbursement amount.    Expenses paid by employee
Timesheet Timesheets store all time entries that are made for a specific member and time period, and approvals can be set up for timesheets. Time entries can be added from the timesheet screen for charge codes, activities, and tickets.  Time card Monthly, Weekly
Work Role Work Roles are a required field for time entries, and they determine the work being performed, the standard billing rate, and the description that displays on the invoice. Agreements can be customized to include/exclude work roles. Base Rate Consulting, Bench Technician, Development, Installation
Work Type Work Types are a required field for time entries, and they modify the Work Role rate for certain types of work being completed. Agreements can be customized to include/exclude work types. Rate Modifier Remote, Onsite, Weekend/Holiday, After Hours
Setup
Customer Portal The Customer Portal is a web based application used by ConnectWise PSA partners and their clients to create new service tickets, monitor the status of requests and incidents, and provide you with access to the Knowledge Base. ConnectWise PSA partners log into the ConnectWise PSA based customer portal, and your customers log into the portal setup within your database. Client Interface  
Group Groups are subsets of locations that enable you to classify your members, and they are often used to describe different types of business units. Groups can be used for security settings and for reporting. Department, Business Unit, Cost/Revenue Centers, Division Admin, Help Desk, Professional Services, AdHoc
Location Locations differentiate the billing sectors of your organization. Each location has it sown groups, service boards, project boards, work roles, and billing setup. Time, products, and expenses entered against separate location MUST be billed separately. Invoice Sector  
Security Role Security Roles control the access and level of access to areas within ConnectWise PSA.
